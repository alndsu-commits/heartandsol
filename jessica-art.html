The Artist

Art made for the people in it.

I'm Jessica — a Texas-based mural artist, painter, and mom of two. My work spans large-scale public murals, fluid acrylic pours, and sculptural commissions, with one constant: I make art that belongs to the space and the people who live in it.

Whether it's a community mural that stops people in their tracks, or a painting above your fireplace that feels like it was always meant to be there, I care about the whole experience — from first conversation to final brushstroke.
