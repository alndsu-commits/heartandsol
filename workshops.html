Questions

Good things to know.

What should I wear? +
Something you don't mind getting paint on! We provide aprons but acrylic paint has a way of finding its way onto things. Old clothes or casual wear is perfect.
What's your cancellation policy? +
Life happens — cancellations made 48+ hours before a workshop receive a full credit toward a future session. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable, but you're welcome to send someone in your place.
Do I need any experience? +
Absolutely not. Most workshop guests have never picked up a paintbrush as an adult — and that's exactly who these sessions are designed for. Jessica meets everyone where they are.
Can I buy a spot as a gift? +
Yes! Purchase a spot normally and simply forward the confirmation email to the lucky person. Or reach out via the contact page for a gift card option.
Where are workshops held? +
Currently in Round Rock, TX. Exact address is provided in your confirmation email after booking. Private events can be held at your chosen venue.
Can I book a private session for my team or party? +
Absolutely — private events are Jessica's favorite! Minimum 8 people. Reach out through the contact page and she'll put together a custom proposal including pricing, themes, and logistics.
